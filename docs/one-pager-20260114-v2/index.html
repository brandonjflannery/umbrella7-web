About Us

Umbrella 7 is an applied machine learning research firm specializing in ML-driven business optimization and internal AI transformation. With over a decade of experience in machine learning and full-stack development, we help enterprises capitalize on the latest advancements in AI technology. Our early adoption of large language models for quantitative trading applications positions us uniquely to guide organizations through their AI transformation journey. All engagements are conducted through word-of-mouth referrals.
